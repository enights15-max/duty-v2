## Usage Recommendations
- Use `Primary Scarlet` for CTA, active tabs, selected pills, focus rings and revenue-positive highlights.
- Keep `Danger` distinct from brand scarlet; error states should feel operational, not branded.
- In dark mode, prefer scarlet over black for active emphasis and keep backgrounds wine/graphite, not pure black.
- In light mode, keep surfaces warm and slightly ivory to avoid clinical white-on-red contrast.
- Reserve `Warm Gold` for premium or payout-related moments, not warnings.

## Suggested Pantone Direction
Closest premium print mood references for exploration:
- Scarlet brand: Pantone 7621 C / 1797 C family
- Deep scarlet: Pantone 7623 C family
- Warm gold accent: Pantone 7555 C family
- Dust rose support: Pantone 7612 C family

## Recommended Split
- Consumer app: Dark-first Scarlet Editorial
- Admin / professional web: Light-first Scarlet Editorial
- Errors: `Danger`
- Revenue / positive completion: `Success`
